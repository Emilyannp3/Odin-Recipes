Mushroom Risotto

Description

arborio rice cooked down with white wine and mushroom

Ingredients

  • arborio rice
  • chicken broth
  • white wine
  • fresh parsley
  • parmesan
  • fresh herbs
  • olive oil
  • mushrooms
  • shallots

Steps

  1. Cook rice with wine and broth
  2. Add all other ingredients together
  3. Serve
